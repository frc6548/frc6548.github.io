About Us

We are the Perry RAMBOTS, FRC Team 6548. Our team was established in 2017, and since we have qualified for worlds 4 times. With 22 members, we work dilligently with the community and team members to support them as much as we can. We also support the enviroment by recycling pop cans and cleaning up scrap that other people leave. This helps fund our team so we can contiune to participate in competitions far and near.
